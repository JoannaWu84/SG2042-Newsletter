# SG2042 Newsletter (2023-12-01 #019)

## Editor's Note

Welcome to the nineteenth issue of the SG2042 Newsletter. We are excited to bring you the great news that SOPHGO has officially joined the OpenCloudOS open-source community in November 2023 and will work together with community partners to foster a thriving open-source operating system community.

## Highlights

+ SOPHGO has signed the Contributor License Agreement (CLA) and officially joined the OpenCloudOS open-source community. In the future, SOPHGO will actively participate in the construction of the OpenCloudOS community, fostering mutual growth with community members and collaborating to build an open, neutral, and inclusive community environment to promote a more diverse and robust collaboration within the operating system ecosystem.

  [Related news](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/fH7vwWoguQUNLMBVMEsTMg)
