# SG2042 Newsletter (2023-12-22 #022)

## Editor's Note

Welcome to the twenty-second issue of the SG2042 Newsletter. It's great to see you again! Hope you enjoy this issue, which is about the 8th Global Edge Computing Conference and the RISC-V Software Porting and Optimization Championship. 

## Highlights

+ On December 16, 2023, the 8th Global Edge Computing Conference was grandly opened in Shanghai! One of the sub-forums, SOPHGO Edge Large Model Application, attracted the enthusiastic participation of many famous enterprise representatives, experts and scholars in the industry. During this session, the participants delivered brilliant speeches and had in-depth discussions on topics such as innovative large model technologies and practical applications in various industries. In addition, SOPHGO was honored with the “2023 Edge Computing Annual Leading Enterprise” award at this conference, showcasing its significant position and remarkable contributions in the field of edge computing.
